About us

Specializing in importing and selling high-quality aggregate and stones

Company introduction

Our primary activity is importing gravel (aggregate) for the needs of concrete and asphalt factories. In order to meet our standards, we collaborate with the best gravel suppliers in the United Arab Emirates. For this reason, we have gained the trust of our clients in the Kuwaiti market. Al Qasr Company is capable of providing materials approved by Kuwaiti authorities that comply with Kuwaiti and international standards. We obtain the necessary certifications that support the highest standards and approvals allowed for use in government and semi-government projects, as well as by the Ministry of Public Works in Kuwait.
